- https://floccus.org/
  - Sync bookmarks & tabs privately
  - remember to disable other sync service like browser native bookmark sync
across browsers and devices
- https://dummyjson.com/
  - mock data
- https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/random-youtube-video/kijgnjhogkjodpakfmhgleobifempckf
  - random youtube video
- https://github.com/jeffvli/feishin
  - A modern self-hosted music player.
- https://pdfsam.org/
  - split and merge pdf
  - works with ADF scanner to do duplex scanning
    - scan front of all pages, save as front.pdf
    - scan back of all pages, save as back.pdf
    - merge front.pdf and back.pdf, check "reverse" option for back.pdf
- https://github.com/keycastr/keycastr
  - KeyCastr, an open-source keystroke visualizer
